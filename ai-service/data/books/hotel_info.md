# Grand Vista Hotel - Complete Information Guide

## Welcome to Grand Vista Hotel

Grand Vista Hotel is a luxurious 5-star resort located in downtown Manhattan, offering world-class amenities and exceptional service to our valued guests.

## Hotel Overview

**Address:** 123 Manhattan Avenue, New York, NY 10001
**Phone:** +1 (212) 555-0100
**Email:** reservations@grandvista.com
**Website:** www.grandvista.com

Grand Vista Hotel features 250 elegantly appointed guest rooms and suites with stunning city views, state-of-the-art facilities, and award-winning dining options.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What's included in the room rate?
Room rate includes accommodations, WiFi access, and use of fitness facilities. Breakfast is not included unless part of a package.

### Can I extend my stay?
Yes, subject to availability. Contact the Front Desk at least 24 hours in advance.

### Do you offer loyalty programs?
Yes, our "Prestige Rewards" program offers points on every stay, redeemable for upgrades and complimentary services.

### What's your refund policy?
Refunds depend on your rate type. See "Cancellation & Refund Policy" section above.

### Are there any hidden fees?
No. All fees (parking, pet fees, resort fees) are disclosed at booking. Only additional services ordered by guests result in extra charges.
