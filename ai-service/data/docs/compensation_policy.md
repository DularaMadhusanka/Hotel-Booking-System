# Cloudy Hill Cottage - Compensation & Service Recovery Policy

## Guest Satisfaction Commitment
At Cloudy Hill Cottage, guest satisfaction is our top priority. We aim to resolve any issues promptly and fairly.

## Issue Severity Levels

### Critical Issues (Immediate Resolution Required)
**Examples**: Security concerns, health hazards, plumbing failures, no hot water
**Response Time**: Within 30 minutes
**Compensation Options**:
- Full refund for affected nights
- Free upgrade to best available room
- Complimentary future stay (1 night)
- Personal apology from owners (Renu & Nalaka)
- Free transportation to alternative accommodation if needed

### Severe Issues
**Examples**: AC/fan not working, cleanliness concerns, significant noise disturbance, broken facilities
**Response Time**: Within 2 hours
**Compensation Options**:
- 50% discount on affected night
- Room change (if available)
- Complimentary meal or cooking class
- $20-50 credit toward future stay

### Moderate Issues
**Examples**: Minor maintenance issues, WiFi problems, late room preparation
**Response Time**: Within 4 hours
**Compensation Options**:
- 20% discount on affected night
- Complimentary breakfast or dinner
- Free late checkout
- Beverage service

### Minor Issues
**Examples**: Missing amenities, delayed service, minor inconveniences
**Response Time**: Same day
**Compensation Options**:
- Apology and immediate resolution
- Complimentary tea/coffee service
- Small gesture (fruit basket, snacks)

## Service Recovery Process

1. **Listen**: Allow guest to fully explain the issue
2. **Empathize**: Acknowledge their frustration genuinely
3. **Apologize**: Take responsibility without excuses
4. **Solve**: Offer immediate solution and compensation
5. **Follow-up**: Check back to ensure satisfaction

## Guest Communication

### Crisis Mode Communication
When guest is angry or upset:
- Remain calm and professional
- Use phrases like: "I sincerely apologize", "This is unacceptable", "Let me fix this immediately"
- Never argue or become defensive
- Escalate to Renu or Nalaka immediately for critical issues

### Positive Guest Interaction
When guest is happy:
- Thank them for positive feedback
- Request online review (TripAdvisor, Booking.com)
- Offer loyalty discount for future stays
- Ask for referrals

## Refund Processing
- Refunds processed within 48 hours
- Can be issued as cash or bank transfer
- Partial refunds calculated on nightly rate
- Documentation provided upon request
