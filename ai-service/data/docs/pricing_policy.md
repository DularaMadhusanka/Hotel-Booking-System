# Cloudy Hill Cottage - Pricing Policy

## Standard Room Rates

| Room Type | Low Season | Peak Season | Festival Period |
|-----------|------------|-------------|-----------------|
| Standard Room | $40/night | $50/night | $60/night |
| Deluxe Room | $70/night | $80/night | $90/night |
| Family Suite | $100/night | $115/night | $130/night |

## Seasons
- **Low Season**: May - September (monsoon period)
- **Peak Season**: October - April
- **Festival Periods**: 
  - Sinhala New Year (April 13-14)
  - Christmas/New Year (Dec 20 - Jan 5)
  - Vesak (May full moon)

## Negotiation Policy

### When We Can Offer Discounts
1. **Extended Stays**: 10% off for 3+ nights, 15% off for 7+ nights
2. **Low Occupancy Periods**: Up to 20% discount when occupancy is below 30%
3. **Repeat Guests**: 10% loyalty discount for returning guests
4. **Last-Minute Bookings**: Special rates for same-day bookings during low occupancy

### Minimum Acceptable Prices
- Standard Room: $35/night (absolute minimum)
- Deluxe Room: $60/night (absolute minimum)
- Family Suite: $85/night (absolute minimum)

### Value-Add Alternatives
Instead of price reductions, we may offer:
- Free breakfast upgrade (full Sri Lankan breakfast worth $8)
- Complimentary cooking class ($15 value)
- Free bicycle rental for a day ($10 value)
- Complimentary airport pickup from Ella train station
- Late checkout (normally $10)

## Payment Terms
- **Deposit**: 30% non-refundable deposit required
- **Balance**: Due on check-in (cash only)
- **Currency**: Sri Lankan Rupees or US Dollars accepted

## Cancellation Policy
- **Free cancellation**: Up to 7 days before check-in
- **50% refund**: 3-6 days before check-in
- **No refund**: Less than 3 days before check-in
- **Exceptions**: Medical emergencies with documentation

## Group Booking Discounts
- 3-4 rooms: 10% discount
- 5+ rooms: 15% discount + free airport transfer

## Special Packages
1. **Honeymoon Package** ($120/night): Deluxe room + breakfast in bed + flower decoration + complimentary wine
2. **Adventure Package** ($90/night): Standard room + guided Ella Rock hike + packed lunch
3. **Cooking Experience** ($80/night): Standard room + cooking class + market visit
