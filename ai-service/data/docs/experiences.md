# Cloudy Hill Cottage - Experiences & Activities

## On-Site Experiences

### 1. Cooking Class with Renu
**Duration**: 3-4 hours
**Price**: $15 per person (free for guests staying 3+ nights)
**Schedule**: Daily at 10:00 AM or 4:00 PM

**What's Included**:
- Visit to local market to buy fresh ingredients
- Learn to make 3-4 authentic Sri Lankan dishes
- Renu's famous Jackfruit Curry recipe
- Coconut sambol, dhal curry, rice
- Enjoy your creations for lunch/dinner

**Highlights**: 
- Hands-on experience with traditional spices
- Learn the secret of perfect Sri Lankan rice
- Take home recipe cards

### 2. Sunrise Breakfast Experience
**Duration**: 2 hours (5:00 AM - 7:00 AM)
**Price**: $20 per person
**Schedule**: Daily (weather permitting)

**What's Included**:
- Wake-up tea/coffee at 5 AM
- Short walk to viewpoint
- Watch sunrise over Ella Gap
- Full Sri Lankan breakfast with a view
- Hot beverages

### 3. Garden Tea Tasting
**Duration**: 1 hour
**Price**: Complimentary for all guests
**Schedule**: Daily at 4:00 PM

**What's Included**:
- Sample 5 local Ceylon teas
- Learn about tea grading and processing
- Traditional tea-time snacks
- Beautiful garden setting

## Nearby Attractions

### Ella Rock Hike
**Distance from Cottage**: 1.5 km to trailhead
**Difficulty**: Moderate
**Duration**: 4-5 hours round trip
**Best Time**: Early morning (5:30 AM start)

**Details**:
- Spectacular 360Â° views from summit
- Trail through tea plantations and forest
- Nalaka provides detailed maps and guidance
- Packed breakfast available ($5)

### Nine Arch Bridge
**Distance from Cottage**: 2 km
**How to Get There**: 25-minute walk or 5-minute tuk-tuk
**Best Time**: 6:00 AM for sunrise, 3:30 PM for train photo

**Details**:
- Iconic colonial-era railway bridge
- Train passes approximately every 2-3 hours
- Beautiful through the tea plantations
- Great photo opportunity

### Little Adam's Peak
**Distance from Cottage**: 3 km
**Difficulty**: Easy to Moderate
**Duration**: 2-3 hours round trip

**Details**:
- Easier alternative to Ella Rock
- Perfect for families
- Stunning views of Ella Gap
- Tea plantation walk

### Ravana Falls
**Distance from Cottage**: 5 km
**How to Get There**: Tuk-tuk ($5) or bus
**Duration**: 1-2 hours

**Details**:
- 25-meter waterfall
- Swimming pool at base (during dry season)
- Cave nearby with Buddhist shrine
- Good for afternoon trip

### Lipton's Seat
**Distance from Cottage**: 20 km
**Duration**: Half-day trip
**Best Time**: Before 10 AM (for clear views)

**Details**:
- Panoramic viewpoint over tea country
- Named after Sir Thomas Lipton
- Tuk-tuk tour recommended ($25-30)
- Stop at tea factory on the way

The famous Kandy-Ella train is a must-do experience!
- **Ticket Price**: ~$3 for 2nd class
- **Book in Advance**: Tickets sell out quickly
- **Most Scenic Section**: Haputale to Ella
- **Duration**: 6-7 hours from Kandy
