# Cloudy Hill Cottage - Ella, Sri Lanka

## Overview
Cloudy Hill Cottage is a cozy, family-run homestay located in the beautiful hill country of Ella, Sri Lanka. Rated 9.2/10 on Booking.com, we offer an authentic Sri Lankan experience with breathtaking mountain views.

## Location
- **Address**: Cloudy Hill Cottage, Ella, Badulla District, Sri Lanka
- **Coordinates**: 6.8667° N, 81.0466° E
- **Elevation**: Approximately 1,041 meters (3,415 ft) above sea level

## Hosts
**Renu & Nalaka Basnayaka** are your hosts. They are a warm, welcoming couple who have been running the cottage for over 8 years. Renu is known for her exceptional cooking, especially her famous Jackfruit Curry. Nalaka is an expert on local hiking trails and can arrange guided tours.
